About Me

Dynamic and result-oriented professional based in Jogeshwari (E), Mumbai — where the creative energy of a city of dreams fuels everything I build. With over 4 years of hands-on experience spanning Graphic Design, Campaign Management, and UI/UX, I sit at the intersection of aesthetics and strategy.

I started my journey at Samco Securities in 2021 as an Email Executive, learning the ropes of high-volume digital communication. That foundation allowed me to grow rapidly — within a year I moved into a Campaign Manager role at Samco Mutual Fund, where I built complete B2B and B2C marketing ecosystems from scratch.

Today, as Assistant Manager — Marketing & Graphic Design, I lead the end-to-end redesign of the Samco Mutual Fund corporate website, combining my Figma UI/UX skills with real user research and conversion optimization thinking. I'm also deeply invested in the AI revolution — integrating Generative AI into our marketing workflows to accelerate production while keeping quality high.

I believe good design is never just decoration — it's communication. Every pixel, every color choice, every layout decision should serve the person on the other end of the screen. That philosophy guides everything I create, whether it's a logo, a landing page, or an email that reaches a million inboxes.
